# Order Parameter ($\Phi$)

## Definition

The Order Parameter ($\Phi$) defines the overall state of the system based on latent variables. It drives transitioning behavior across the Team State Machine.

In V1, it was computed as the weighted sum of all Execution conditions. 
**In V2, the order parameter is multi-dimensional:**
$$\Phi(t) = [\Phi_V, \Phi_P, \Phi_E, \Phi_A, \Phi_L]$$

Each sub-score is a normalized composite of its block's sub-variables. The **overall system state** is determined by the minimum sub-score (because of the [Master Equation's](../legacy/master-equation.md) multiplicative constraint), but the **pattern** of sub-scores determines which interventions will work.

## State Transitions (P1-P5)

| State | Pattern | Characteristics | Primary Risk |
|-------|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **[P1 Crisis](p1-crisis.md)** | Any $\Phi < 0.3$ | Losing loop active; fires everywhere. | Irreversible attrition and debt. |
| **[P2 Stabilizing](p2-stabilizing.md)** | Worst $\Phi$: 0.3-0.5 | Fragile; fires reducing. | Regression if load increases. |
| **[P3 Functional](p3-functional.md)** | All $\Phi$: 0.5-0.7 | Delivery works; limited improvement. | Slow decay without investment. |
| **[P4 Thriving](p4-thriving.md)** | All $\Phi > 0.7$ | Winning loop engaged; calm. | Complacency; decay of invisible conditions. |
| **[P5 Scaling](p5-scaling.md)** | $\Phi_E$ high, $\Phi_A/\Phi_P$ declining | Growth breaks coordination and culture. | A and P cannot keep pace with E demands. |

## Extended V2 States (Mismatch States)

Because $\Phi$ is multi-dimensional, a system can have high execution but low value, or high execution but low people health. See [V2 Extended States](v2-extended-states.md) for the 5 named mismatch patterns (Wrong Thing Fast, Sweatshop, etc.).

## [P6 Mixed State](p6-mixed-state.md) Rule 

Because of the multi-dimensional architecture, you must never average sub-scores across blocks. A team with $\Phi_P = 0.2$ and $\Phi_E = 0.9$ is not a "0.55 team." It is a high-executing team about to collapse due to burnout and fear. You must intervene exactly at the block driving the lowest score.
