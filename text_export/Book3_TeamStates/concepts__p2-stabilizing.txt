# P2 — Stabilizing

## Definition

The team is exiting operational Crisis ([Losing Loop](losing-loop.md)). Fires are reducing but the situation is fragile.

## Symptoms

- Flow is returning but not consistent
- Direction holds for one cycle at a time
- Basic quality practices are resuming

## Intervention (Execution Layer)

- Hold Direction/Protection levers steady (priority 4/5 each)
- Add basic Standards
- Do not overload with new processes yet

## Review Cadence
1×/week

## Transitions

- → [P3-Functional](p3-functional.md): Delivery predictable for 3+ cycles, ownership clear, basic standards holding
- → [P1-Crisis](p1-crisis.md): Priority churn returns, hidden work resurfaces, quality bypasses resume

## V2 Multi-Dimensional Threshold & Hysteresis

In Canonical V2, P2 describes the Execution sub-vector in the $0.3 \leq \Phi_E < 0.5$ range. 

Because of [Hysteresis](hysteresis.md), P2 is highly fragile. Dropping below $0.25$ pulls the team back into P1. In this fragile state, the system is hyper-sensitive to external shocks from other blocks. A sudden shock to Strategy ($\Delta V$) or a re-org constraint ($\Delta A$) will shatter P2 instantly, plunging the team into a [V2 Extended State](v2-extended-states.md) collapse.
