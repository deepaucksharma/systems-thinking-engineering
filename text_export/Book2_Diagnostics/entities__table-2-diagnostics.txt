# Table 2 — Diagnostics (V2.1)

## Purpose

Table 2 is the diagnostic engine of the framework. It maps **observed symptoms** to **root cause diagnoses** and prescribes the correct **lever** to pull, reflecting the updated [20 - Diagnostic Sequence](../concepts/20-diagnostic-sequence.md) while preserving the older [Fix-Order](../legacy/fix-order.md) lineage for comparison.

## Diagram 6: The Comprehensive V2.1 Decision Tree

In V2.1, macro-environmental elements (Value, People, Org) act as gates on execution. The diagnostic tree first checks for no-win states, then verifies People-system integrity, then diagnoses local Execution, and only then performs a deeper Org inspection if execution keeps relapsing.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    START(["Symptom Observed\nBegin Diagnosis"])

    %% Step 0: no-win conditions
    MACRO_ZERO{"STEP 0\nAny no-win condition\nnear zero?"}
    START --> MACRO_ZERO

    MACRO_ZERO -->|"YES: Value / People"| FIX_PV["Apply Zero-Override\nStabilize strategy or people first"]
    MACRO_ZERO -->|"YES: Hard Org blocker"| FIX_A0["Escalate structural blocker\nDo not promise local rescue"]
    MACRO_ZERO -->|"NO"| PEOPLE{"STEP 1\nIs People-system telemetry\ntrustworthy enough to proceed?"}

    %% Step 1: people integrity
    PEOPLE -->|"NO"| FIX_PV
    PEOPLE -->|"YES"| E_ZERO{"STEP 2\nIs any Block E condition\nnear zero?"}

    %% Step 2: execution
    E_ZERO -->|"YES"| WHICHZERO{"Which condition\nis near zero?"}

    WHICHZERO -->|"Clarity"| FD1["Pull Direction Lever\nRewrite goal"]
    WHICHZERO -->|"Focus"| FD2["Pull Protection Lever\nCut WIP"]
    WHICHZERO -->|"Quality"| FD5["Pull Standards Lever\nTighten gates"]
    WHICHZERO -->|"Skill or Coord"| SVSD{"Structure vs\nDevelopment Rule"}

    SVSD -->|"Cannot tell"| FIXSTR["Fix Structure"]
    SVSD -->|"Skill gap"| FIXDEV["Fix Development"]

    E_ZERO -->|"NO (Apply Order)"| C1{"Order 1: Clarity"}
    C1 -->|"NO"| FC1["Pull Direction Lever"]
    C1 -->|"YES"| C2{"Order 2: Focus"}
    C2 -->|"NO"| FC2["Pull Protection Lever"]
    C2 -->|"YES"| C3{"Order 3: Skill / Coord"}

    C3 --> SVSD2{"Distinguish skill\nfrom ownership?"}
    SVSD2 -->|"No / Coord"| FS2["Pull Structure Lever"]
    SVSD2 -->|"Skill confirmed"| FD3["Pull Development Lever"]

    FS2 --> C4
    FD3 --> C4

    C4{"Order 5: Quality"}
    C4 -->|"NO"| FC4["Pull Standards Lever"]
    C4 -->|"YES"| L_CHECK{"Block L\nFeedback fast enough?"}

    %% Step 3: learning then deeper org inspection
    L_CHECK -->|"NO"| FC5["Pull Feedback Speed Lever"]
    L_CHECK -->|"YES"| ORG_ALIGN{"STEP 3\nDo E problems persist\nafter local fixes?"}

    ORG_ALIGN -->|"YES"| FIX_A["Inspect A in depth\n(IA, DR, XT, TC, TT)"]
    ORG_ALIGN -->|"NO"| HEALTHY(["Enter Manager Loop"])

    FC1 & FC2 & FC4 & FC5 & FD1 & FD2 & FD5 & FIX_PV & FIX_A0 & FIX_A & FIXSTR & FIXDEV --> LOOP
    LOOP(["Wait 2 full cycles\nObserve leading indicators"])
```

## V2.1 Extended Diagnostic Rows

| ID | Symptom | Block | Root Cause | Lever |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **V1** | Team ships fast but users ignore it | Value ($V$) | Strategic Drift / weak Customer Value | Strategy / Hypothesis |
| **P1** | Secret blockers; exhaustion | People ($P$) | Fear / Burnout (PS or Su near zero) | Safety / Load Reduction |
| **A1** | Working hard but blocked externally | Org ($A$) | Inverse Conway / bad incentives / blocked decision rights | Escalate / Re-org |
| **D1** | Internal confusion about the local goal | Exec ($E_C$) | Direction failure (Clarity) | Direction |
| **D3** | Lots started, little finished | Exec ($E_F$) | Flow failure (Focus) | Protection |
| **D5** | Engineers stuck on technical problems | Exec ($E_S$) | Capability failure (Skill) | Development |
| **D6** | Internal work collides or breaks | Exec ($E_{Co}$) | Coordination failure | Structure |
| **D7** | Bugs reaching users; fragile system | Exec ($E_Q$) | Standards failure (Quality) | Standards |
| **L1** | Same incident happens three times | Learn ($L$) | Slow signal (Feedback Speed / poor learning loop) | Batch Size / CI-CD / Retros |

## Embedded Rules & Theorems

- **[Zero-Override Rule](../legacy/zero-override-rule.md):** Overrides everything, especially when Value or People health drops out.
- **[Structure-vs-Development Rule](../concepts/structure-vs-development-rule.md):** Disambiguate macro causes before diagnosing local execution skill gaps.
- **[P6 - Mixed State Rule](../concepts/p6-mixed-state.md):** Never average sub-scores across blocks when diagnosing.
- **[V2 Theorems](../concepts/v2-theorems.md):** Underpin this logic via the *Measurement Reliability Theorem* (check $PS$ before trusting metrics) and the *Escalation Theorem*.
