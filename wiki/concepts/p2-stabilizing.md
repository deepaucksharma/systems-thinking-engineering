# P2 — Stabilizing

## Definition

The team is exiting the [Losing Loop](losing-loop.md). Fires are reducing but the situation is fragile.

## Symptoms

- Flow is returning but not consistent
- Direction holds for one cycle at a time
- Basic quality practices resuming

## Intervention

- Hold Direction/Protection levers steady (priority 4/5 each)
- Add basic Standards
- Do not overload with new processes yet

## Review Cadence

1×/week

## Transitions

- → [P3-Functional](p3-functional.md): Delivery predictable for 3+ cycles, ownership clear, basic standards holding
- → [P1-Crisis](p1-crisis.md): Priority churn returns, hidden work resurfaces, quality bypasses resume

## Mathematical Threshold & Hysteresis
In the [Extended Framework](../sources/3-extended-framework.md), P2 exists in the $0.3 \leq \Phi < 0.5$ range of the [Order Parameter ($\Phi$)](order-parameter.md). 
Because of [Hysteresis](hysteresis.md), P2 is highly fragile. Dropping below $0.25$ pulls the team back into Crisis, requiring them to reach $0.35$ to pull back out again.
