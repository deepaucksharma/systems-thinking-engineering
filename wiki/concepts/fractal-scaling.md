# Fractal Scaling

## Definition

The framework exhibits a fractal property: the five conditions (Clarity, Focus, Skill, Coordination, Quality) and the manager's control loop apply systematically at every scale of human organization.

## Diagram 10: Fractal Scaling Diagram

| Condition | Micro (Team) | Meso (Department) | Macro (Ecosystem / Region) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Direction** | Sprint goal | OKRs & Roadmap | Gov. IT Policy (e.g. GCC 4.0) |
| **Protection** | WIP Caps | Portfolio Limits | Infrastructure, Power, Real Estate |
| **Development** | Pairing | Eng. Ladders | Universities, Tech Communities |
| **Structure** | Named Owners | Team Topologies | Data Centers, Tech Parks |
| **Standards** | CI/CD Gates | Arch Review Boards| Regulatory Framework, IP Law |
| **Feedback** | Retrospectives | QBRs | Academia ↔ Industry loops |

## Application

Because the thermodynamic rules scale infinitely:
- The [Losing Loop](losing-loop.md) runs at every scale.
- A zero condition collapses output identically, whether it's a team lacking a sprint goal or an economic region lacking an industrial policy.
