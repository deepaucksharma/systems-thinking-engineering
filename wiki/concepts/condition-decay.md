# Condition Decay (Principle 7)

## Definition

All conditions in the [Master Equation](../entities/master-equation.md) **always decay** without active maintenance. This is labeled Principle 7 in the framework.

## Implication

The [Winning Loop](winning-loop.md) is not self-sustaining. Even when a team reaches [P4-Thriving](p4-thriving.md), complacency or maintenance neglect will erode Focus, Skill, Coordination, and Quality back toward lower states.

## What Decays

- **Slack** — gets filled with new commitments
- **Skill** — atrophies without practice, erodes with turnover
- **Coordination** — degrades as team composition changes or scope shifts
- **Quality** — standards erode under pressure ("just this once")

## Operational Consequence

Every P4 row in the playbook includes "don't get complacent" because decay is the default trajectory. Maintenance is not optional — it is the cost of staying in the winning loop.
