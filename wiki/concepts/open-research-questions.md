# Open Research Questions

## Definition

The Engineering Management Control Theory framework remains an active research program. Its elevation to predictive science exposes boundaries and unproven hypotheses that require further empirical testing.

## Known Unknowns

1. **Optimal Condition Balance:** Is there a "golden ratio" between Clarity, Focus, Skill, Coordination, and Quality ($C:F:S:Co:Q$), or is optimization strictly governed by localized contexts?
2. **Causal Ordering Dynamics:** Do highly integrated condition interdependencies mean that pushing Skill *first* automatically strengthens the system's ability to defend its own Focus?
3. **Temporal Half-lives:** What is the precise decay rate of specific conditions? Without physical reinforcement, does Clarity decay in 2 days? 14 days?
4. **Individual Differential Tolerance:** Do exceedingly high-skill Principal Engineers natively tolerate lower Focus (higher thrash) dynamically, or is the Context-Switch cognitive penalty a universally hard structural barrier regardless of experience?
5. **Cross-Cultural Mapping:** Does the [Cynical Equilibrium](cynical-equilibrium.md) model map 1:1 globally, or do Hofstede's cultural dimensions fundamentally alter systemic incentives in non-Western management teams?
6. **Psychological Safety Integration:** Should Psychological Safety be mapped mathematically as an explicit 6th multiplier dimension, or does it exist purely as a secondary latent proxy supporting [Skill](skill.md) (pairing openness) and [Quality](quality.md) (fearless code review)?

## Future Expansions

- **Machine Learning Diagnosis:** Training specialized classification models directly against the [Table 2 Diagnostics](../entities/table-2-diagnostics.md) map using pure Jira/GitHub telemetry to auto-diagnose constraint collapses.
- **Reinforcement Learning Policies:** Deploying localized AI agents into the [Digital Twin](digital-twin.md) simulations to discover entirely novel intervention lever strategies.
