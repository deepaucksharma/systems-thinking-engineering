# P4 — Thriving

## Definition

The [Winning Loop](winning-loop.md) is fully engaged. The team is calm, improving, and trusted. Slack exists and is actively invested.

## Symptoms

- Team improves while delivering
- Throughput increases without chaos
- Standards are maintained and gradually raised
- Morale is high

## Intervention

- Maximize Development (priority 5/5)
- Raise Standards gradually (priority 5/5)
- **Don't get complacent** — [Condition Decay](condition-decay.md) is always active

## Review Cadence

Monthly

## Transitions

- → [P5-Scaling](p5-scaling.md): Rapid hiring, scope expansion, major reorg
- → [P3-Functional](p3-functional.md): Complacency, standard erosion, improvement time consumed
- → [P1-Crisis](p1-crisis.md): Rapid growth without structure, key person leaves without transfer, stakeholder panic + scope flood

## Mathematical Threshold
In the [Extended Framework](../sources/3-extended-framework.md), P4 exists in the $0.7 \leq \Phi < 0.85$ range of the [Order Parameter ($\Phi$)](order-parameter.md). It operates like a ferromagnetic state with compound growth.

## Phase Space Trajectory (Diagram 8)
P4 sits in the upper-right quadrant of the Phase Space Diagram. The primary risk from P4 is moving inward toward the center due to scaling stress, or drifting toward the [Hero Culture Trap](hero-culture-trap.md) through complacency.
