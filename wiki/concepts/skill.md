# Skill (OM3)

## Definition

The third condition in the [Master Equation](../entities/master-equation.md). Measures whether the team can solve the technical problems they face. Moved by the **Development** lever.

## Leading Indicators

- Pairing frequency
- Review depth
- Skill coverage breadth (bus factor)

## Lagging Indicators

- Time to solve problems
- Repeated failures on similar issues
- Bus factor (single points of expertise)

## If Near Zero

Engineers are stuck. Blockers accumulate. Problems that should take hours take weeks.

## Lever: Development

- Pair with a stronger engineer
- Narrow the problem scope
- Targeted coaching
- Show results from coaching before asking for budget

## Disambiguation

See [Structure-vs-Development Rule](structure-vs-development-rule.md): when you can't tell if the problem is Skill or [Coordination](coordination.md), fix Structure first — coordination failures mask true skill levels.
