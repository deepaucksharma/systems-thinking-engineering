# P5 — Scaling

## Definition

The [Winning Loop](winning-loop.md) is running but at risk. Growth is stressing existing structure — hiring, scope expansion, or reorg is creating friction that didn't exist before.

## Symptoms

- Structure starts creaking
- Onboarding bottlenecks appear
- Dependency explosion
- What worked at smaller scale no longer works

## Intervention

- Redesign Structure (priority 5/5)
- Re-clarify Direction (priority 5/5)
- Strengthen onboarding

## Transitions

- → [P4-Thriving](p4-thriving.md): New structure stable, onboarding repeatable, delivery predictable post-growth
- → [P2-Stabilizing](p2-stabilizing.md): Growth without ownership reset, key people leave, dependency explosion

## Mathematical Threshold & Conway's Law
In the [Extended Framework](../sources/3-extended-framework.md), P5 occurs when $\Phi \geq 0.85$ but its derivative $\frac{d\Phi}{dt} < 0$. The structure is under immense stress. This occurs precisely when [Conway's Law](conways-law.md) constraints are violated by team size scaling past architectural boundaries.
