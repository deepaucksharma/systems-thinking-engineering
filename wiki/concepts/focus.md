# Focus (OM2)

## Definition

The second condition in the [Master Equation](../entities/master-equation.md). Measures whether the team can work without fragmentation. Moved by the **Protection** lever.

## Leading Indicators

- WIP within cap
- Fewer interruptions
- Maker hours (uninterrupted blocks)

## Lagging Indicators

- Cycle time
- Task aging (how long items sit in progress)
- Throughput

## If Near Zero

Nothing finishes. WIP piles up. The team context-switches constantly. This is the entry point of the [Losing Loop](losing-loop.md).

## Lever: Protection

- Cut WIP immediately
- Cancel noise (unnecessary meetings, low-priority requests)
- Batch requests instead of streaming them
- Protect maker time

## Connection to Slack

Focus > 0 creates **slack** — the surplus capacity that enables the [Winning Loop](winning-loop.md). Without focus, there is no slack, and the winning loop cannot engage.
